## âš ï¸ Troubleshooting

### Device Not Found
- Ensure watch is on and in HR broadcast mode
- Move closer to the device
- Try scanning again

### Connection Failed
- Make sure device isn't connected to another app
- Turn Bluetooth off/on on your phone
- Restart the Polar watch

### Heart Rate Shows "--"
- Check watch is on wrist and tight enough
- Ensure HR sensor is enabled on watch
- Wait a few seconds for reading to stabilize

### Workout Auto-Pauses
- Devices may be out of range
- Check all watches are still broadcasting
- Move closer to the devices
